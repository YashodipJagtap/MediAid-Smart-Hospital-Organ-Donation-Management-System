About HealthConnect

Welcome to HealthConnect, a platform designed to transform healthcare accessibility. We bring hospitals, patients, and emergency services together to provide efficient and timely medical care, especially in critical situations. Whether you're searching for a nearby hospital, requesting an ambulance, or locating an organ bank, HealthConnect ensures that you have seamless access to essential healthcare services.

  • Real-time hospital locator based on your location.
  • Emergency ambulance dispatch for quick response in case of accidents or health crises.
  • Disease prediction by analyzing symptoms with advice on care.
  • Blood and organ bank integration to support hospitals in need of life-saving resources.
  • Doctor directory, showing all doctors registered in hospitals on our platform.
  • First aid tips and home remedies for managing diseases based on symptoms.
